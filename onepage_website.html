~ About Banff National Park ~

 
 

~ Accomodations~

Cabins

Experience the serenity and quintessential Canadian charm of waking up in a log cabin in Banff National Park. The ultimate pick for a cozy retreat, a visit to one of the many cabins and chalets in Banff and Lake Louise is ideal for a peaceful long weekend escape or romantic getaway.


    

Hotels

There’s a wide range of places to stay in Banff National Park, where you’ll be spoiled for choice when it comes to unique locations, breathtaking views and luxurious amenities. With so many hotels to choose from, you’re sure to find the ideal spot to call home base during your stay here.


Bed & Breakfast

Experiencing a cozy bed and breakfast in Banff and Lake Louise brings a uniquely warm and personal element to your stay. Each bed and breakfast in the area has its own distinct charm and character, run by owners that are delighted to share their love of Banff National Park with guests. During your visit, you’re sure to pick up their favourite tips on the best spots to eat, shop, and play - a special perk of staying with the friendly locals.


    

Camping

Camping in Banff and Lake Louise is a great way to immerse yourself in the stunning scenery of Banff National Park. Frontcountry sites offer the joys of sitting by a campfire and relaxing near the lake with the convenience of full amenities and washrooms close by. Adventurous spirits who crave the experience of sleeping under the stars in remote places will enjoy one of the many backcountry Parks Canada campsites. For the comforts of home on wheels, there are a number of full-service campgrounds where you can park and plug in your recreational vehicle.
